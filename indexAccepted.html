Energy XR

EPRI

Energy XR uses Extended Reality (XR) to help explore complex concepts in transmission, distribution, and advanced telecommunications by using augmented, mixed, and virtual reality. The 3D substation above is placed partially in Virtual Reality (VR) in this page and then is placed in a live video feed of the environment in the next by using Augmented and Mixed Reality (AR/MR). As a graphical user interface, it may also be used to monitor and control systems from a mobile device or headset.

iOS

The Mozilla WebXR viewer may be downloaded by pressing 'Get WebXR App'.

After downloading and opening WebXR, (1)press 'Launch XR from WebXR App' button again. (2) Touch outside of the AR Notification and (3) Enable usage of device motion and access to the camera.

Android

This mobile only prototype works on Firefox WebXR (iOS) and Chrome and Firefox on Android due third party software limitations. Please check your browser by using the button below before entering Extended Reality.
